About Meal Planner

Meal Planner is an online platform that helps you plan and organize your meals. With Meal Planner, you can:

  • Create customized meal plans for the week
  • Explore a variety of recipes and save your favorites
  • Generate shopping lists based on your meal plans
  • Track your nutrition and dietary goals
